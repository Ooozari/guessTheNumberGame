Rules and Guidelines

  1. Enter a number between 1 - 100.
  2. You have a total of 10 attempts to guess the number.
  3. After each guess, you'll receive feedback whether your guess is too high or too low.
  4. If you guess the correct number within 10 attempts, you win the game!
  5. If you don't guess the number in 10 attempts, you lose the game and the correct number will be revealed.
  6. Hints will be provided after every incorrect guess to help you get closer to the correct number.
  7. Guesses must be within the range of 1 to 100. Any guess outside this range will not be counted.
  8. You can restart a new game at any time by clicking the 'New Game' button.
  9. Your number of attempts and the time taken to guess correctly will be recorded as your score.
